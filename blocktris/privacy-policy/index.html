Privacy Policy

Slice Tower / 슬라이스 타워

This privacy policy explains how Slice Tower handles information for the Android app published under package name com.clawler.slicetower.

Effective date: 2026-04-17

1. What the app does

Slice Tower is a single-player arcade game. It is designed to be playable without creating an account, signing in, or submitting personal profile information.

2. Data stored on your device

The current app behavior stores a small amount of gameplay record data locally on the device so the game can remember values such as your last recorded height, best recorded height, and cumulative total height between sessions. This data is written to the app's local save storage on the device and is not designed to be synced to a server by the current game code.

This local save data stays on the device unless you remove the app, clear the app's storage through Android settings, or the operating system removes the app data.

3. Data the app does not intentionally collect

Based on the current app implementation, Slice Tower does not intentionally collect or transmit personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone input, or advertising identifiers from within the game itself. The current repository also does not include in-game account creation, chat, analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or in-app purchase SDKs.

4. Permissions, network access, and platform services

The Android app manifest currently declares INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE, and WAKE_LOCK. These are manifest-declared Android permissions included by the current build template and engine setup. They are not presented to the user as Android runtime permission prompts.

The current game is intended to function as an offline single-player game. Based on the current implementation, the game code does not ask for camera, microphone, contacts, photo library, or precise location access, and it does not implement user accounts, chat, ads, analytics, or in-app purchases.

If you download the app through Google Play, Google may process technical or diagnostic information under its own policies when handling downloads, updates, app signing, crash reporting, device compatibility, or store operations. Those platform-level processes are outside the direct control of Slice Tower.

5. Children

Slice Tower is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children or adults through in-app forms or account systems. If you believe app data has been handled in a way that conflicts with this policy, contact the publisher so the issue can be reviewed.

6. Changes to this policy

This page may be updated if the app behavior changes, for example if online services, analytics, ads, or account features are added in the future. The effective date at the top of this page will also be updated when material changes are published.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, requests, or notices about Slice Tower, email doctor-clawler@gmail.com .
